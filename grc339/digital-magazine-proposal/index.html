Introduction

Sonder

Sonder is the realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own. This magazine focuses on exposing projects and upcoming brands of the youth in California when it comes to art, fashion, music, photography, film, writing, and sport. Anecdotal articles featuring compelling interviews and showcasing ideas. Advertisements for art sales, music shows, film exhibitions, and more. Providing a space for young people to spread awareness for their passions and how they are making a difference in their community through their work.
