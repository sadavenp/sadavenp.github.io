Introduction

The Early Bird

The Early Bird is a bakery located in the heart of San Francisco that provides top notch cookies, breads, and pastries to locals and visitors. The bakery also offers drinks such as coffee, matcha, and teas to complete the menu.

Target audience

Locals and Tourists, most likely adults

Locals who know about the shop and are looking to place an online order or browse the menu for a refresher. Tourists who are visiting the city may use the website to find the location, hours, and what kind of goods are offered before visiting in person.

About

Nestled in the heart of North Beach San Francisco since 1954, The Early Bird is a bakery and pastry shop that most locals consider a hidden gem. Founded and owned by a local family, we welcome familiar faces and new ones. Enjoy our signature pastry and hot coffee on an early foggy morning with us!
